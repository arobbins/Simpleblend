Front-end Development done right.

Many times the front-end is left to developers who simply aren't passionate about it. Corners are cut, leaving sites with fragile CSS and poor JavaScript performance. This leads to maintainability issues as the site grows costing money and headaches down the road. I take special care in making the right decisions in the beginning so this doesn't happen. How should we handle responsive images? Should we support browsers or features? What kind of build system should we use? I love what I do, and always keep quality in mind.
